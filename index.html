SHYAM KRISHNAN K

Researcher / Doctoral Student

Shyam is a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Doctoral fellow in DigitAlgaesation Network. He is a Researcher at Proviron Industries and a PhD student at the University of Antwerp, Adrem Data Lab, supervised by Prof Dr Kris Laukens and Dr Luc Roef. He is developing data mining approaches for decision support of PBR processes. The research focuses on developing predictive models for monitoring and optimizing algae production and growth conditions. Besides, shyam holds a Master's in BigData Analytics and AI and a Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology. He was co-founder of a startup named Nestor and worked as a product manager in the Electrical service, Production and manufacturing domain.
