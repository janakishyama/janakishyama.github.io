SHYAM KRISHNAN K

Researcher / Doctoral Student

Shyam is a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Doctoral fellow in DigitAlgaesation Network. He is a Researcher at Proviron Industries and a PhD student at the University of Antwerp, Adrem Data Lab, supervised by Prof Dr Kris Laukens and Dr Luc Roef. He is developing data mining approaches for decision support of PBR processes. The research focuses on developing predictive models for monitoring and optimizing algae production and growth conditions. The key objective is to extract the industrial data stored by SCADA systems and use it for the growth optimization of microalgae using machine learning and data mining practices. Besides, shyam holds a Master's in BigData Analytics and Artificial intelligence and a Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology. He was co-founder of a startup named Nestor and worked as a product manager in the Electrical service, Production and manufacturing domain. Shyam is a passionate kayaker. Above all, he is a cat & book lover and a good storyteller. He lives in the beautiful city of Antwerp, where his biking skills are growing as fast as his favorite algae Rhodomonas-salina.
